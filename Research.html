Research



Research is very important for any project that we work on because it provides us more knowledges and ideas related to our project. My research contains website of different header, footer, hover effects on navigation bar and icons. Well, I have done many researches to complete my portfolio website but the idea from the following five websites were really important than the rest to make my portfolio website look professional.



Research no.1

The first thing I did to start my project was creating a header with fixed navigation bar. In my website, when we are in a particular section of the navigation bar, that section turns in crimson colour to indicate that we are in that section. We can see that the "Blog section" is in crimson color since we are in "Blog" webpage. In all the pictures below of 5 researches, the left picture is that of the website where I did my research on and the right picture is the picture of my website after getting ideas from that particular website. The link of the website is:
http://greenfieldbelser.com/blog/johos-following-a-coffee-bean-story



Research no.2

To make my navigation bar attractive, I used hover effect on it by getting ideas from "Feed" website. When I place the cursor on a particular section of the web page of my website, a line comes at the top and bottom of that particular section as shown below. Therefore, the "Feed" website was helpful to make the navigation bar attractive by adding hover effect. The link of the website is:
https://shop.feed.co/uk_en



Research no.3

The third research I did was to make a contact form. I did many researches on internet and found Islington College's contact form and used the idea to make a contact form. I also inserted a map in the contact page as shown below. The link of the website is:
http://islington.edu.np/contact/



Research no.4

The next research I did was on "Foodmood" website. I made a footer containing my contact information, email, address and social icon links in the footer like in the "Foodmood" website footer as shown below. The link of the website is:
http://www.foodmood.com.np



Research no.5

Since, there were no hover effect on the social icons of "Foodmood" website and my website, I looked for the footer of other different websites as well. I liked the hover effect on icons in the footer of "ETQ" website and therefore, I used this idea and added hover effect on my social icons in the footer. As we can see, when I place my cursor on the social icon, the color changes to green and the icon rotates exactly 20 degrees. The link of the website is:
https://www.etq.com
